The Scholastic Policy

Prime Minister Narendra Modi maintains a strict personal policy of not accepting honorary degrees. He has declined honorary doctorates from Southern University (2014), Banaras Hindu University (2016), and Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda, emphasizing his preference for work over honorary titles.
